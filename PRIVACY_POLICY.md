# FormReceipt Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024-01-01

## Data We Process
- Google Form responses: used only for PDF generation, never sent to external servers
- Email addresses: used only to send receipt emails
- License key: stored in Script Properties for Pro authentication

## Data Sharing
FormReceipt does not share your data with third parties. All processing runs in your Google Account's Apps Script environment.
